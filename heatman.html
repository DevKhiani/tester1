A fiery industrial stage with lava pits, flame jets, and a long disappearing‑block section that demands tight timing.

Heat Man — a fiery Robot Master who charges up and dashes across the room in blazing bursts.

🔧 Weakness

Heat Man is weak to the Bubble Lead, which cuts through his fire dash easily. He also takes solid damage from the Air Shooter if you need a quick backup.
