⚙️ The Stage

Quick Man’s stage is a high‑speed gauntlet built inside a towering energy facility filled with deadly instant‑kill laser beams. The level constantly forces you to react fast: dropping shafts, branching paths, and tight corridors keep the pressure on.

🤖 The Boss

Quick Man — a hyper‑fast Robot Master who dashes around the arena and attacks with sudden rushes. His speed makes him one of the toughest bosses to hit consistently.

🔧 Weakness

Quick Man is weak to the Time Stopper, which freezes him and drains half his health instantly. He also takes solid damage from the Crash Bomber if you need a backup option..
