FLASH MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

⚙️ The Stage

A chilled, metallic maze with branching paths, conveyor floors, and turret enemies. Slippery ice panels and shifting platforms keep you moving as you navigate the blue‑lit corridors.

🤖 The Boss

Flash Man — a Robot Master who freezes time with Time Stopper and peppers the room with rapid buster shots while pacing back and forth.

🔧 Weakness

Flash Man is weak to the Metal Blade, which cuts through his slow movement and tight room layout. He also takes good damage from the Crash Bomb if you want an alternate option.
