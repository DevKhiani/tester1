METAL MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

⚙️ The Stage

Metal Man’s stage is a fast-paced conveyor-belt factory filled with gears and crushers. Moving floors drag you toward danger while Moles and Screw Drivers keep the pressure high.

🤖 The Boss

Built for precision, Metal Man throws lethal Metal Blades. The shifting floor in his room makes his patterns a nightmare to dodge if you aren't quick on your feet.

🔧 Weakness

He falls quickly to the Quick Boomerang. Ironically, he is also his own worst enemy—a single Metal Blade in the rematch will take him down instantly.
