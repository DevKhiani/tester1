⚙️ The Stage

An underwater cavern filled with waterfalls, high‑jump water physics, and spike‑lined ceilings. Enemies like frogs, snails, and angler‑fish crowd the path, and careful movement is key to avoiding instant‑kill spikes.

🤖 The Boss

Bubble Man — a slow underwater Robot Master who hops and fires Bubble Lead..

🔧 Weakness

Bubble Man is weak to the Metal Blade, which shreds through his low mobility. He also takes strong damage from the Quick Boomerang.
